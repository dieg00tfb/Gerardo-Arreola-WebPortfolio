Privacy Dots

A motion detector (a Seeed Studio XIAO ESP32C6 equipped with a motion sensor connected) that sends real-time alerts to a Raspberry Pi, serving as a central hub for smart home devices to connect. The Raspberry Pi, running a Django server, pushes notifications to a dedicated mobile application, providing users with a comprehensive platform to view and manage all their alerts seamlessly.

Leadership

As the team lead for Privacy Dots, I guided a group of four in designing and developing
a motion detection system that seamlessly integrates with a mobile app to deliver real-
time notifications. Over the course of the project, I managed all aspects of planning,
task delegation, and system integration, ensuring timely delivery and exceptional
performance. The product was showcased at the Louisiana Tech First-Year Projects
Showcase, where we presented its innovative design and real-time capabilities to the
judges. Our project earned First Place, highlighting the success of our collaborative
efforts and the practical impact of our solution.

Technology and Tools Used

AML-S905X-CC (Le Potato)

The Raspberry Pi serves as the central hub, connecting all devices and managing alerts to the mobile app. It also operates as the Django server host.

Seeed Studio XIAO ESP32C6

Configured with firmware to interface with a motion sensor for real-time detection, sending push notifications via Django server.

AM 312 Mini PIR Motion Sensor

The motion sensor is connected to the Seeed Studio XIAO ESP32C6, monitoring for motion.
